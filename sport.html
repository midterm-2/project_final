ONLINE TRAINING FOR EVERY DAY

HOW ARE CLASSES GOING?

Classes are held in Zoom, you don't need to download or connect anything. Prepare a fitness mat, a comfortable uniform and a bottle of drinking water for the start of the class. Check your internet connection. At the specified time, click on the link and start training. Follow the coach's instructions. You can ask a question and get comments from the coach.

  • The best back exercise you can do anywhere without any equipment

    If you are looking for an exercise with your own weight to strengthen your back muscles - consider that you have found it.

  • Don't ignore the warm-up

    Despite the lack of time, pay enough attention to the initial and final stage. In order not to injure the joints, always train in sneakers. Eliminate physical activity within an hour after eating. Training for beginners should last no more than 20-25 minutes.

  • Recommendations to follow during classes

    Take care of the free space for training. In close quarters, the risk of injury increases many times. Special sports shoes and clothing will also help to avoid injury. Pants and a T-shirt should not be too loose or, on the contrary, excessively narrow. During the illness, do not exercise. This is the most important rule.
